Namaskar Shloka

Angikam Bhuvanam Yasya
Vaachikam Sarva Vaangmayam
Aahaaryam Chandra Thaaraadee
Thum Numas Saathvikam Shivam.


Meaning:
We bow to that Lord Shiva, whose body is the whole universe, whose speech is the entire world's languages and whose ornaments are the stars and moon.

Seven Dance Styles

Bharatanatyam from Tamilnadu
Kathak from Uttar Pradesh
Kathakali from Kerala
Manipuri from Manipur
Odissi from Orissa
Mohini Attam from Kerala
Kuchipudi from Andhra Pradesh


Meaning of BhaRaTaNatyam
Bha stands for Bhava meaning Expressions.
Ra stands for Raga meaning Music.
Ta stands for Tala meaning Rhythm.
Thus, it is a mixture of expressions, music and rhythm through dance.
