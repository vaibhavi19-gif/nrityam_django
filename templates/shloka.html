Shloka


Angikam Bhuvanam Yasya
Vaachikam Sarva Vaangmayam
Aahaaryam Chandra Thaaraadee
Thum Numas Saathvikam Shivam.
Namo Namaha
Namo Namaha
Namo Namaha



Meaning:
We bow to that Lord Shiva, whose body is the whole universe, whose speech is the entire world's languages and whose ornaments are the stars and moon.
